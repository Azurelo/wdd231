Ollie

A fundamental trick where the rider leaps into the air with the skateboard without using their hands.

Kickflip

Heelflip

Similar to the kickflip, but the skateboard flips in the opposite direction using the heel.

Pop Shuvit

A trick where the skateboard spins 180 degrees horizontally beneath the rider while staying in the air.

Varial Kickflip

Combines a pop shuvit and a kickflip, causing the board to rotate horizontally while flipping.

Manual

A balancing trick where the rider lifts the front wheels off the ground and rides on the back wheels.

Nosegrind

Involves grinding along an edge or rail with the front truck while the back truck is elevated.

Boardslide

The rider slides the middle of the board along a rail or ledge while facing perpendicular to the obstacle.
